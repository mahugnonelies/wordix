Data Deletion Policy — Wordix

Last update: 2025-01-01 • Contact: support@wordixapp.com

1. Data concerned

  • Anonymous identifier generated by the app
  • Game progress and statistics
  • Push notification token
  • Minimal technical history related to your session

2. Request procedure

Send an email to support@wordixapp.com with the subject “Account deletion”.

3. Deletion timelines

Deletion within 30 days maximum. Some technical data may be retained temporarily for legal reasons.

4. Irreversibility

⚠️ Deletion is final and irreversible.
